corruption-o-bot

A live tracker of political stock trades — House, Senate & the President — with party/office filters, a performance view (return since each trade vs. the S&P), a best-traders leaderboard, a Democrats-vs-Republicans head-to-head, and corporate-donor cross-referencing. Built to feed buy/sell signals toward a trading layer.
